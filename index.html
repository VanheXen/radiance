How do I get my link or UID?

Wish-history link (Windows) — fetches fresh data:

  1. Start Genshin on your PC and open Wishes → History.
  2. Open PowerShell (search "PowerShell" in the Start menu).
  3. Run this, then copy its output (your wish link): iwr -useb stardb.gg/wish | iex
  4. Paste that link above and press Fetch.

UID — after importing once:

  1. Do the link import above at least once (here or on stardb.gg/wish).
  2. Then just type your 9-digit UID (bottom-right in-game) and press Fetch.

On mobile or console? The link step needs a PC — import once on a PC, then use your UID here (or Load JSON). On PC but not Windows? Use the stardb exporter or stardb.gg/wish.

Disclaimer & privacy — how your data is handled

Estimate, not truth

The Capturing Radiance counter uses an unofficial community model — HoYoverse has never published the real mechanic. Results are estimates and can be wrong, especially on partial histories.

Your data — how you load decides what's exposed

This app only ever contacts stardb.gg (for wishes) and genshin-db (character names — no personal data).
  • Load JSON — nothing is sent; your wish data stays on your device.
  • Link / UID (Fetch) — require internet and go through stardb. Pasting a link imports your history to stardb, which then makes it publicly readable by anyone who knows your UID (stardb's policy). UID just reads that public data. stardb's private toggle does not hide it — only deleting your wishes on stardb does.

The extract script is safe by itself

Getting your link (stardb.gg/wish or stardb-exporter) only reads your local game cache and validates the link against Hoyo's servers — it does not upload anything. Exposure happens solely when you hand that link to stardb (its website or this app's Fetch).

Offline & fully private

Only Load JSON works without a connection (character data is built in). To stay private: get a JSON from a local exporter, then Load JSON — your data never touches stardb. (Link and UID can't be used offline.)

Authkey

A wish link holds a read-only key that expires in ~24h and is sent only to stardb.
